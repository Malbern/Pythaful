## Lists
Different types and layouts of lists.

### Ordered List
Numbers the list, what comes after 1 doesn’t matter.

    1.
    *
    -
    +
 
1) 1
 * 2
  - 3
+ 4

***
### Unordered List
Just have dots making up the list.

    *
    - -
    +
    * [x]
    - [ ]
    + [ ] 
    
* 1
- - Outer
* [x] Checked
 * [ ] Unchecked
  * [ ] Unchecked

***
### Ordered & Unordered List
A combined type of list that has numbers and dots.
