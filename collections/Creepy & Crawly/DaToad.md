####Crawlie -
The crawler of your data and everything related. It roams your file systems and lets you automatically or manually do things such as, discover hierarchies(Still learning!), which parts of the file system you have access to, recursively explore and look for different pathways, lists where issues may be found, and more than a browser of your files… (When you need to have advanced browsing. You can have certain details turned on or off. 

It acts as a manager, find something whether it be automatically or manually and it will signal that there’s a match, organize and see all files of one type. Write, read, copy, paste, transfer, duplicate, copy from and to, delete, have a deletion feature that doesn’t fully delete the file.
